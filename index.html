About Me

I am very passionate about web developing and design, it simultaneously challenges me, intrigues me and motivates me. My magnificent obsession began a couple years ago. I sat in front of my computer I followed the instructions, not quite able to visualize my destination and then I typed into the text editor - it felt like I didn't understand how everything was linked but I was determined to complete the task. I have to admit, I was used to seeing the brush strokes and the colours in a creation but then we opened the document in our browser and I was amazed! The page had brilliant colours, the fleshy images, the bold words, in that moment it gave life to something so beautiful. I created more pages, I played around with the commands, I googled other ways of creating moving parts and found an array of creations. I took more courses & watched more tutorials to learn to take the ideas in my head and make them real.
